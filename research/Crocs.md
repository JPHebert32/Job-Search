## What they do
Crocs, Inc. (NASDAQ: CROX) designs and sells casual footwear globally under two brands: the flagship **Crocs** brand (foam clogs and sandals) and **HEYDUDE** (casual lifestyle shoes acquired in 2022 for $2.5B).

## Business model
Wholesale + direct-to-consumer retail: sells through department stores, shoe retailers (DSW, Foot Locker), and direct via Crocs.com/HEYDUDE.com. Revenue mix is shifting toward DTC and international. Branded content and collaborations (Lego, Disney, etc.) drive premium demand. Two-brand portfolio with different margin profiles.
