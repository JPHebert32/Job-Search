**Prepared:** May 14, 2026

## Executive Summary

NMI (Network Merchants) is a privately-held payments enablement platform processing $502B+ in annual payment volume across 6.5+ billion transactions. B2B SaaS infrastructure — they power ISOs, ISVs, and payment facilitators, not direct merchants. Strong fintech domain alignment with JP's MWD Trading background. Power BI + Tableau in the stack.

---

## 1. What NMI Does

**White-label payment gateway OS** — software companies and payment facilitators build branded payment solutions on NMI's infrastructure.

- ~6,000 channel partners | 1.2M+ active merchants
- $502B+ annual payment volume | 6.5B+ transactions annually
- Omni-channel: online, in-store, mobile, self-service, unattended

**Key Products:**
- Payment Gateway (core) — white-label, 200+ processor integrations, 125+ shopping carts
- Merchant Central — payments CRM, sales/lead management, automated risk, instant onboarding
- Network Tokenization — Customer Token Vault, iSpyFraud, ScanX AI fraud detection
- Value-Added: Text-to-Pay, Bill Connect, Tap to Pay, Shopify integration

## 2. Business Model

## 8. Interview Talking Points

### Lead With

1. **T-Life KPI Framework** — "I built the HEART KPI framework from scratch for a 75M-download app and owned 30 dashboards consumed by VP and C-suite daily. NMI's product analytics needs map directly to that work."

2. **MWD Trading financial data background** — "I spent 9 years analyzing transaction-level financial data — P&L, position reporting, performance analytics for Finance. Settlement reconciliation and ledger accuracy aren't new territory for me."

3. **Scale comfort** — 6.5B transactions annually is large but JP has worked at similar volume scales in telecom and trading.

4. **Data quality** — T-Sentiment reconciliation processes and T-Life anomaly detection directly map to "financial data integrity across transaction datasets."

### Questions to Ask

- "The job mentions a data analytics guild — how does analytics collaborate across product, finance, and marketing?"
- "What are the top 3 product KPIs you're tracking in 2026 and where are the current visibility gaps?"
- "Network Tokens and Shopify are fast-growing extensions — what analytics exist around adoption and partner ROI for those products?"
- "How is the team handling settlement reconciliation analytics at this scale?"

### Watch Outs

- NMI is B2B2B — frame analytics experience in terms of partner/operator metrics, not consumer UX
- Don't lead with consumer analytics framing (T-Life downloads) — reframe around KPI frameworks, product adoption, and financial data integrity
- BigQuery/dbt gap — be honest, position as adjacent and fast to ramp

### Comp Strategy

- Floor is $90K, ceiling is $120K — JP's target is $125K+
- Get in the room, demonstrate value, negotiate at offer stage
- Leverage: 10+ years experience vs. 3-year minimum requirement, financial domain depth, Power BI expert
