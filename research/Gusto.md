## What they do
Gusto is a B2B SaaS platform providing payroll, HR, and benefits management for small and medium-sized businesses — serving 500,000+ businesses, or roughly 1 in 12 US businesses.

## Stage & funding
- **Late-stage private:** $9.3B valuation (June 2025 tender offer), $718M raised across 9 rounds ([Crunchbase](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/gusto))
- **IPO candidacy:** Widely speculated; no confirmed timeline. Late-stage, not yet public.
- **Employees:** ~4,369 (as of March 2026)
- **No recent layoffs:** Unlike many 2024–2026 tech layoffs, Gusto has been stable in headcount

## Business model
Subscription + transaction SaaS: SMBs pay per-employee per-month for payroll processing, plus add-on subscriptions for benefits administration, HR tools, compliance, and Gusto Money (financial services). Revenue scales with customer headcount and product attach rate.
