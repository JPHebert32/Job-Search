# Recharge — Senior Data Analyst, GTM Analytics

## Quick Take
Strong apply — the comp band ($152K–$190K) well exceeds JP's target, the role is fully remote, and the GTM analytics scope aligns with senior-level BI work. The main gap is tool stack: Recharge runs Looker + dbt as hard requirements, and JP's primary tool is Power BI; Looker exposure is the key gap to address in positioning. The domain (subscription ecommerce / B2B SaaS) is a solid fit and avoids all of JP's deal-breaker categories.

---

## Company
- **Stage/funding:** Series B, $277M total raised; valuation ~$2.1B (Bain Capital Ventures, Summit Partners, Iconiq Capital as lead investors)
- **Size:** ~289 employees as of late 2023 (13% headcount decline from 2022 peak — layoffs noted)
- **Product:** Subscription commerce platform powering recurring revenue for DTC/ecommerce merchants; features include customer portals, churn prevention, bundles, loyalty, analytics, and SMS. Processes $30B+ in recurring revenue for 20,000+ businesses (clients include Dr. Squatch, quip, Blueland, Bobbie)
- **Industry:** B2B SaaS, subscription payments/ecommerce tech
- **Culture notes:** Headquartered in Santa Monica, CA; fully remote company. Core values: ownership, empathy, day-one, humility. Glassdoor mixed — 72% recommend, 3.5/5 WLB. Positive: smart team, blameless engineering culture, good remote setup. Negative: CEO described as volatile (executives frequently fired, functions scrapped/rebuilt); culture has eroded somewhat in recent years. Acquired Giftcloud (April 2025). Revenue ~$113M (2025).

## Role
- **Title:** Senior Data Analyst, GTM Analytics
- **Location:** Remote (US)
- **Comp:** $152,000–$190,000 USD + medical/dental/vision, retirement with employer contribution, flexible PTO, paid parental leave, remote and merchant stipends
- **Team:** Reports into central Data & Analytics team; embedded partnership with GTM org (Sales, Business Development, Revenue Operations, Demand Generation)
- **Key responsibilities:**
  - Build and maintain dbt models powering GTM reporting (pipeline, funnel, conversion, bookings, forecasting)
  - Design dimensional models and metric definitions for "one version of truth" datasets
  - Develop Looker dashboards and explores enabling self-serve analytics across GTM leadership
  - Implement data quality checks and documentation
  - SQL/Python analysis to identify trends and diagnose GTM performance
  - Collaborate with GTM stakeholders to translate ambiguous business questions into metrics and recommendations
  - Build AI-powered data products using LLMs (metric explainers, natural-language Q&A, automated insights)
  - Work independently in fast-paced, ambiguous environment
- **Tool stack required:** dbt (hard requirement), Looker (hard requirement), SQL (advanced), Snowflake, Python; LLM/AI tool experience also required

## Fit Assessment

### Strengths (JP's profile vs role)
- **10+ years analytics experience** easily meets the 5-year minimum; senior-level scope matches
- **Advanced SQL** is a direct match — core skill cited throughout
- **Snowflake** experience is a direct match (platform listed as required)
- **GTM analytics domain** — pipeline, funnel, conversion, forecasting work is transferable from product analytics; stakeholder-facing dashboard work (VP/C-suite audience) is a demonstrated strength
- **Self-serve analytics design** — T-Life solo ownership of 30 dashboards with 100+ daily VP/C-suite visits is highly relevant positioning for "scalable self-serve insights" framing
- **Python (intermediate)** covers the preferred Python qualification
- **B2B SaaS preference** is a direct match; subscription/ecommerce is a reasonable adjacent domain
- **Comp** — floor of $152K is $37K above JP's preferred target; this is a standout offer
- **Remote, no RTO, no on-call, no crypto/defense/gambling** — all clean
- **dbt** — JP has dbt on profile; if hands-on experience is real, this partially covers the analytics engineering requirement

### Gaps
- **Looker** — JP has not used Looker; this is the single biggest gap. Recharge lists it as a hard requirement with specific depth expected (explores, scalable self-serve design). Need to address in application: position Power BI dashboard architecture experience as conceptually equivalent and note ability to ramp quickly
- **Analytics engineering depth in dbt** — role requires dimensional modeling, testing, documentation, version control workflows in dbt. JP should honestly assess depth of hands-on dbt experience vs. exposure
- **Payments/ecommerce domain** — listed as preferred (not required), but JP has no payments background; DTC subscription is a new vertical
- **LLM/AI tooling** — listed as a requirement; JP should surface any AI-assisted analytics work in application

### Deal-breaker flags
- None hard. The Looker gap is a yellow flag — it's a must-have in the JD — but the overall comp, remote setup, and domain make it worth applying and letting the interview process reveal how flexible they are on tool ramp. CEO instability (Glassdoor signal) is worth watching but not a pre-apply blocker.
