## Company Context

Stellantis is the parent of Jeep, Ram, Dodge, Chrysler, and ~10 other brands. HQ: Amsterdam, with North American ops centered in Auburn Hills, MI.

**2026 Situation — Turnaround in Progress:**
- Took a $26.5B write-down in Feb 2026 to unwind overextended EV investments
- Pivoting back to ICE/V-8 for core brands (Ram, Dodge muscle cars)
- Canceling plug-in hybrid Jeep and Chrysler models
- Jeep Grand Cherokee sales down 11% YoY — active recovery effort underway
- Manufacturing layoffs ongoing (900 tariff-related, Ohio Jeep plant cuts)
- **Implication:** Analytics and performance measurement are MORE critical during a turnaround, not less. This is a "fix the business with data" moment.
