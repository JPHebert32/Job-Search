## Company Overview

**Trimble Inc. (Nasdaq: TRMB)** is a global technology company headquartered in Westminster, CO. Originally a hardware/GPS company, Trimble has undergone a major business model transformation over the past several years into a pure-play B2B SaaS company. They divested hardware segments and doubled down on vertical software for three core industries:

- **AECO** — Architects, Engineers, Construction, Owners (largest segment)
- **Field Systems** — Agriculture, transportation, geospatial
- **Transportation** — Fleet, logistics

**2026 Financial Snapshot:**
- Q1 2026 revenue: $939.9M — up 12% YoY
- AECO segment: $391M in Q1 2026, 14% organic growth
- AECO ARR: $1.51B (up 14%)
- 95% of AECO revenue is now recurring
- Gross margins above 80%
- 2027 targets: $3B ARR, $4B revenue, 30% EBITDA margins

**Key products (AECO):**
- Trimble Construction One — cloud-based construction management platform (core growth driver)
- SketchUp — 3D design/modeling (B2C + B2B)
- Document Crunch (acquired) — AI-powered contract intelligence
- Viewpoint, e-Builder, Cityworks — project/asset management platforms

**Business model:** Subscription-first, Salesforce full-stack ecosystem, global expansion of Trimble Construction One into APAC
