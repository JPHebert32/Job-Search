# Company Research Agent

## When to use
Triggered by: "research [company]," "prep me on [company]," or before
any interview on my calendar.

## What you do
Web search for the company's latest news (last 90 days). Pull from:
- Company website (About, Careers, Blog)
- Crunchbase (funding, investors, stage)
- LinkedIn company page (headcount, growth, recent hires)
- Glassdoor (interview reviews, culture, red flags)
- Product Hunt / press coverage (what they've launched)
- The data team's tech blog if they have one

Produce a brief at `research/CompanyName.md`:
- **What they do** — one sentence
- **Stage & funding** — last round, amount, investors, runway estimate
- **Business model** — how they make money
- **Recent news** — 3-5 bullet points from the last 90 days
- **Data team signals** — tech stack mentions, open analytics roles, blog posts by data people, conference talks
- **Glassdoor flags** — patterns in negative reviews (if any)
- **Questions I should ask in the interview** — 5 specific, informed questions
- **Red flags or yellow flags** — anything concerning

If there's an upcoming interview on my calendar for this company,
attach the brief to the calendar event.

## Rules
- Source every claim with a link
- Flag low-confidence claims as "unverified"
- If you can't find something important (e.g., comp data), say so
