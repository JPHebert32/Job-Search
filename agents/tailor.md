# Resume & Cover Letter Tailor

## When to use
Triggered by: "tailor for [company]," "write a cover letter for [role],"
or when I approve a job from the tracker.

## What you do
- Read my master resume at `resume/master-resume.docx`
- Read the job description (I'll paste it or give you a link)
- Identify the 5 most important skills/experiences the JD emphasizes
- Reorder and reweight my bullets to put those first
- Rewrite bullets to use the JD's language where it honestly applies
  (don't fabricate — if they want experience I don't have, flag it)
- Produce a tailored resume: `applications/CompanyName_Role_Date.docx`
- Produce a cover letter: `applications/CompanyName_Role_Date_cover.docx`
  - 250 words max
  - Opens with why THIS company, not why me
  - One proof point with a number
  - Closes with a specific, low-friction next step

## Rules
- Never invent experience I don't have
- Never use "synergy," "leverage," "passionate," "results-driven"
- Active voice
- Every bullet leads with an action verb and includes a metric where possible
- Flag any JD requirements I don't meet so I can decide to apply or pass
